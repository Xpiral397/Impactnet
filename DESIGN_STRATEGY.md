## üìê SECTION-BY-SECTION STRATEGY

### HERO SECTION - "THE HEART"

**Inspiration**: Charity: Water's emotional photography approach

**Design Elements**:
1. **Full-Screen Hero Image/Video**
   - Real person being helped (high-quality photography)
   - Option for background video showing actual impact
   - Subtle overlay gradient (not too dark)

2. **Headline - Emotional Impact**
   - "Every Life Matters. Every Gift Creates Hope."
   - NOT numbers-focused initially
   - Speaks to hearts, not wallets

3. **Sub-headline - The Mission**
   - "Transforming communities across Nigeria through faith-driven transparency"
   - Clear, human-focused language

4. **Primary CTA**
   - Large, prominent: "Change a Life Today"
   - Secondary: "See Our Impact" (not "View Ledger")

5. **Visual Elements**:
   - NO emojis
   - Professional icons from Lucide React
   - Smooth scroll indicator
   - Subtle animation (not distracting)

**Photography Requirements**:
- Real Nigerian families/children
- Professional quality (no stock photos that look fake)
- Respectful, dignified portrayal
- Shows hope, not just poverty
